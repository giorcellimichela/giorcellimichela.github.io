About

MG

Michela Giorcelli is a Professor of Economics at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). She is a Faculty Associate at the NBER and a research affiliate at CEPR.

Prof. Giorcelli is an economic historian studying how managerial and technological innovations affect firm productivity. Her work has been published in leading economic journals, including the American Economic Review, the Journal of Political Economy, and the Review of Economic Studies.
